Conversation Types

CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Technology, or senior operating roles where execution systems, product leadership, and AI capability matter.

Advisory and consulting

Product, architecture, delivery, AI adoption, execution quality, or operating-model questions where you need a technical operator, not generic advice.

Talks, workshops, panels, mentoring, or educational conversations around software, AI, systems thinking, and leadership execution.

Writing, product thinking, research, or serious conversation where clarity, systems, and implementation matter more than noise.
