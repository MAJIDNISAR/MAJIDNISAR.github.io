<div class="project-vision reveal reveal-up">
  <p class="project-vision-text">
    We are moving away from the era of <strong>Source Code</strong> and entering the era of <strong>Direct Manifestation</strong>.
  </p>
  <p>
    By leveraging high-reasoning LLMs, KUN generates optimized machine-level binary directly from natural language —
    bypassing human-readable programming languages and traditional compilers entirely.
  </p>
  <p>
    If you can think it, it exists.
  </p>
</div>

<section class="project-architecture reveal-stagger">
  <h2 class="section-heading">The Architecture</h2>
  <div class="arch-cards">

    <div class="arch-card arch-card--prophet card-lift">
      <div class="arch-card-number">01</div>
      <h3 class="arch-card-title">The Prophet</h3>
      <span class="arch-card-subtitle">Intent Engine</span>
      <p>Translates human "Vibes" and logic into a mathematical <strong>Intent Graph</strong> — a formal representation of behavior that can be reasoned about computationally.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="arch-card arch-card--weaver card-lift">
      <div class="arch-card-number">02</div>
      <h3 class="arch-card-title">The Weaver</h3>
      <span class="arch-card-subtitle">Binary Emitter</span>
      <p>Maps the Intent Graph directly to CPU-specific Instruction Set Architectures — <strong>x86, ARM, RISC-V</strong>. No intermediate representation. No compiler pass. Pure binary.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="arch-card arch-card--sentinel card-lift">
      <div class="arch-card-number">03</div>
      <h3 class="arch-card-title">The Sentinel</h3>
      <span class="arch-card-subtitle">Verification Layer</span>
      <p>A formal logic layer that ensures the generated binary is <strong>safe, memory-secure, and accurate</strong> to the original prompt. Trust, but verify — formally.</p>
    </div>

  </div>
</section>

<section class="project-how-it-works reveal reveal-up">
  <h2 class="section-heading">How It Works</h2>
  <div class="hiw-steps reveal-stagger">
    <div class="hiw-step">
      <span class="hiw-step-number kicker">01 · Declare</span>
      <p>Describe the system's behavior in <strong>text, voice, or logic diagram</strong>. Natural language is the interface.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="hiw-step">
      <span class="hiw-step-number kicker">02 · Synthesize</span>
      <p>KUN performs a <strong>neural-pass</strong> to find the most efficient execution path through the possibility space of valid programs.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="hiw-step">
      <span class="hiw-step-number kicker">03 · Manifest</span>
      <p>Raw machine code is emitted and executed instantly. <strong>No .cpp, no .py, no javac.</strong> Intent becomes binary.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="project-inspiration reveal reveal-up">

    <p>إِنَّمَا أَمْرُهُ إِذَا أَرَادَ شَيْئًا أَن يَقُولَ لَهُ كُن فَيَكُونُ</p>
    <cite>His command, when He wills a thing, is only to say "Be" — and it is. — Quran 36:82</cite>

  <p class="project-inspiration-note">
    KUN is named after the Quranic concept of divine creation through intent alone — the idea that the gap between
    will and existence can be zero. Applied to software engineering: the gap between human intent and machine

  </p>
</section>
