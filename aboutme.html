I build and lead technology work at the intersection of engineering, AI implementation, delivery management, and cybersecurity. My work has spanned product development, architecture, project execution, digital transformation, and academic teaching.

What I Do

I work on turning business needs into practical technology systems. That includes shaping software initiatives, leading delivery teams, improving execution discipline, and bringing AI capabilities into real-world workflows without losing sight of reliability, security, and maintainability.

I am especially interested in the part of technology leadership that sits between strategy and implementation: the place where architecture, delivery planning, stakeholder alignment, and engineering quality meet.

Professional Themes

  • Software engineering leadership across web, platform, and business application delivery.
  • AI integration and implementation with a focus on usable outcomes rather than hype.
  • Cybersecurity-aware systems thinking across architecture, process, and operational decisions.
  • Program and project execution for teams that need both technical depth and delivery rigor.
  • Thoughtful communication through writing, teaching, and mentoring.

Background

Over the years I have worked across software engineering, solutions architecture, project and delivery management, and technology leadership roles. I have also taught computer science and information technology, which continues to shape how I explain systems, mentor teams, and think about building capability in others.

That combination of industry execution and academic experience gives my work a particular bias: practical systems, structured thinking, and clear articulation.
